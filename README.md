# Sales-Customer-Dashboards-using-Tableau

📖 Introduction

This project outlines the specifications and implementation of two Tableau dashboards—Sales Dashboard and Customer Dashboard—to help stakeholders (sales managers, executives, and marketing teams) analyze sales performance and customer behavior.
📊 Sales Dashboard
🎯 Purpose

Provide an overview of sales metrics and trends to analyze year‑over‑year performance and identify key sales patterns.
🔑 Key Features

    KPI Overview: Total sales, profits, and quantity (current vs. previous year).

    Sales Trends: Monthly KPI breakdown with clear highlights of peak and low months.

    Product Subcategory Comparison: Compare sales and profit across product subcategories.

    Weekly Trends: Weekly sales and profit with averages, highlighting above/below‑average weeks.

👥 Customer Dashboard
🎯 Purpose

Offer insights into customer data, trends, and behaviors to support segmentation and improve satisfaction.
🔑 Key Features

    KPI Overview: Total customers, sales per customer, and total orders (current vs. previous year).

    Customer Trends: Monthly KPI breakdown with peak and low months highlighted.

    Customer Distribution: Distribution of customers by number of orders, showing loyalty and engagement.

    Top 10 Customers by Profit: Ranked list with details (orders, sales, profit, last order date).

🎨 Design & Interactivity

    Dynamic Dashboards: Flexible year selection for historical analysis.

    Navigation: Easy switching between dashboards.

    Interactive Charts: Filter data directly through charts.

    Data Filters: Product (category, subcategory) and location (region, state, city).

🛠️ Tools & Skills

    Tableau (dashboard design, interactivity, KPI visualization)

    Excel (data preparation and integration)

    Business analysis (translating user stories into technical requirements)

🚀 Impact

This project demonstrates the ability to:

    Translate business requirements into actionable dashboards.

    Provide stakeholders with clear, interactive insights into sales and customer performance.

    Support decision‑making through data storytelling and visualization.
